Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What services do you offer?

A: Visit our services page for more info!

Q: What are your hours of operation?

A: Here at Paw Friends Grooming Services we have no set hours. We are extremely flexible and do our best to work around your schedule!

Q: Do you groom other pets as well besides dogs?

A: While we don't specialize in other pets we do make accomodations. Please reach out through our contact page and we can answer specific questions.

Q: How much does it cost for my pet to be groomed?

A: Pricing depends on the height, weight, size, and furriness of your little friend. An exact price will be determined prior to your appointment.

Q: How long does it take to groom my pet?

A: Once again, this depends on how much fur needs to be trimmed. An exact price will be determine when you reach out and schedule an appointment.

Q: My dog can be aggressive with strangers, can they still be groomed?

A: Our groomers have experience with all types of dogs, even not so friendly ones. We will do our best to work with you and your dog. Although, this may entail a training process where we slowly introduce them to the grooming environment.

Q: How often should my pet be groomed?

A: Grooming frequency depends on the length and type of your dog's fur. After your first grooming appointment we are more than happy to set up a grooming schedule based on your dog's needs!
